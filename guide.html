Spur Gear — Key Formulas

Pitch Circle Diameterd = m × z
Outside Diameterda = m × (z + 2)
Root Diameterdf = m × (z − 2.5)
Base Circledb = d × cos(α)
Center Distancea = m(z₁+z₂)/2
Addendumha = 1.0 × m
Dedendumhf = 1.25 × m
Whole Depthh = 2.25 × m
Circular Pitchp = π × m
Tip Clearancec = 0.25 × m

Power, Torque & Speed

Torque (N·m)T = P×9549.3/n
Power (kW)P = T×n/9549.3
Angular velocityω = 2πn/60 rad/s
Tangential forceFt = 2T×1000/d (N)
Radial forceFr = Ft×tan(α)
Normal forceFn = Ft/cos(α)
1 HP (metric)= 0.7355 kW
1 HP (imperial)= 0.7457 kW

Pressure Angle Selection Guide

14.5°Legacy standard. Weak root, quiet mesh
20°ISO standard. Best balance of strength & smoothness
25°Heavy load applications. Stronger root, more noise

Planetary Gear Ratios

Ring teeth ruleZr = Zs + 2 × Zp
Fixed ring → carrieri = 1 + Zr/Zs
Fixed carrier → ringi = −Zr/Zs
Fixed sun → carrieri = Zr/(Zr+Zs)
Assembly condition(Zs+Zr) / N_planets = integer
Typical planetary ratios: 3:1 to 10:1 per stage. Two-stage planetary can achieve 100:1.
