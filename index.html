Quick Reference — Standard Modules (ISO 54)
Module (m) is the fundamental size parameter of a gear tooth. A larger module means larger, stronger teeth. Standard modules follow a preferred number series — always select from this table for interchangeability. Pitch Circle Diameter = m × z  |  Addendum = m  |  Whole Depth = 2.25m
